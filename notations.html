Basic Notations



For beginners, it's recommended to know these 5 notations
F (front), B (back), R (right), L (left), D (down), U (up)
Letter followed by ' (apostrophe) means to do the opposite

Basic Algorithms


What is an algorithm?

An algorithm is a set of instructions for solving a problem or accomplishing a task.
One common example of an algorithm is a recipe, which consists of specific instructions for preparing a dish or meal.


There are 7 basic algorithms

  • F' U L' U'
  • D L D' L'
  • D' R' D R
  • U R U' R' U' F' U F
  • U' L' U L U F U' F'
  • F R U R' U' F'
  • R U R' U R U U R'
  • U R U' L' U R' U' L
  • R' D' R D
