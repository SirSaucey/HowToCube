What is a Rubik's Cube?

Rubik's cube is a puzzle in the shape of a cube with 6 faces.
Each face has a different color. Usually that is White, Red, Green, Blue, Orange, Yellow.
Each face of the cube is made up of nine faces of the same color.
In total 54 surfaces are visible on the outside.


Who invented it?

Erno Rubik is a Hungarian inventor and professor born 13 July 1944
He invented the "Magic Cube" in 1974 and more alike puzzles
